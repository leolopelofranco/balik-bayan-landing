    Every day, thousands of people send packages around the world commonly through different established courier companies. Unfortunately most of the time, the cost of sending these packages like documents exceed the cost of the packages.

    Through our site, people can enlist their trips and become an individual courier. With the use of references, social network connections and a verification system, people can trust one another to send their packages and cut the excessive costs with the assurance that their packages arrive.
